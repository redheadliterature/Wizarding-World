Famous Wizarding Spells

  • Expelliarmus
    Charm
    Disarms your opponent by forcing them to release whatever they are holding.
  • Stupefy
    Charm (Stunning Spell)
    Stuns a target, rendering them unconscious temporarily.
  • Lumos
    Charm
    Creates light at the tip of the caster's wand to illuminate dark areas.
  • Accio
    Summoning Charm
    Summons objects towards the caster from a distance.
  • Protego
    Shield Charm
    Creates a magical barrier to deflect spells and physical entities.
  • Alohomora
    Unlocking Charm
    Unlocks doors and other locked objects.
  • Wingardium Leviosa
    Charm
    Levitates objects, allowing the caster to move them through the air.
  • Sectumsempra
    Dark Curse
    Causes deep slashing wounds on the target, bleeding profusely.
  • Expecto Patronum
    Charm (Patronus Charm)
    Conjures a Patronus, a magical guardian that repels Dementors.
