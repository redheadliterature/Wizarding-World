Quidditch

The Magical Sport of the Wizarding World

About Quidditch

Quidditch is a magical sport played on broomsticks by witches and wizards. It is played on an oval-shaped pitch with three goalposts at each end. The game features four balls: the Quaffle, two Bludgers, and the Golden Snitch.

Rules of Quidditch

The game consists of seven players per team: three Chasers, two Beaters, one Keeper, and one Seeker. The objective is to score by throwing the Quaffle through the opposing team's hoops, while avoiding Bludgers and trying to catch the Golden Snitch for 150 points.

Hogwarts Quidditch Teams

Each Hogwarts House has its own Quidditch team, and the teams compete against each other in the Hogwarts Quidditch Cup each year. The four teams are:

  • Gryffindor
  • Slytherin
  • Hufflepuff
  • Ravenclaw

Broomstick Recommendations
