Famous Wizarding Schools

Explore the renowned magical institutions of the wizarding world.

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
Founded by Godric Gryffindor, Salazar Slytherin, Helga Hufflepuff, and Rowena Ravenclaw, Hogwarts is the most famous school of magic in Britain. It houses four houses: Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, and Hufflepuff.
Beauxbatons Academy of Magic
Located in the Pyrenees Mountains of France, Beauxbatons is known for its elegance and grace in the magical arts. Students are trained in refined magic, dance, and charm work.
Durmstrang Institute
Located in northern Europe, Durmstrang has a reputation for producing skilled and aggressive witches and wizards, particularly in the dark arts and martial magic.
Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
The most famous school of magic in North America, Ilvermorny is located on Mount Greylock in Massachusetts. It is known for its four houses: Thunderbird, Wampus, Horned Serpent, and Pukwudgie.
Castelobruxo*
Located in the Amazon rainforest in Brazil, Castelobruxo trains witches and wizards who specialize in magizoology and the care of magical creatures.
Mahoutokoro*
Situated on the volcanic island of Minami Iwo Jima in Japan, Mahoutokoro is known for its rigorous academic programs and for producing exceptional Quidditch players.
Uagadou*
The largest wizarding school in Africa, Uagadou is located in the mountains of Uganda. Known for its expertise in astronomy, alchemy, and the art of casting spells without wands.
