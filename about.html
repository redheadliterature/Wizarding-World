About the Wizarding World Fan Site

Welcome, witches and wizards, to this magical corner of the web dedicated to the enchanting world of Harry Potter! Here you can explore your favorite characters, learn spells, and even discover which Hogwarts house you belong to with our Sorting Quiz.

This fan site is created with love by fellow Potterheads who cherish the magic, friendship, and adventures from J.K. Rowling's Wizarding World. Whether you’re a brave Gryffindor, a cunning Slytherin, a wise Ravenclaw, or a loyal Hufflepuff, there’s something here for everyone.

Stay awhile and explore, cast a spell or two, and maybe send us a message with your own magical stories or questions!

Gryffindor
