Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

Home to the Four Houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. A school for young witches and wizards to learn magic and history.

Beauxbatons Academy of Magic

A prestigious school in France, known for its elegance and grace. Beauxbatons' students are highly skilled in Charms and elegant magical practices.

Durmstrang Institute

Located in Northern Europe, Durmstrang is known for its emphasis on martial magic and Dark Arts studies. It has a strong and proud tradition of producing formidable wizards.
