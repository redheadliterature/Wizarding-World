20 Magical Creatures of the Wizarding World

Phoenix

Hippogriff

Basilisk

Acromantula

Gigantic, talking spider—Aragog lived in the Forbidden Forest; eight‑footer with venomous bite. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Thestral

Centaurs

Half‑horse, half‑human; highly intelligent, skilled at healing and astrology, live in Forbidden Forest. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Merpeople
